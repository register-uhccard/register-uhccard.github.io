How to Register

  1. Visit activate.uhc.com
  2. Click "Register Now"
  3. Enter your Member ID, ZIP Code, and DOB
  4. Create your login credentials
  5. Set up security questions and verification

How to Sign In

Go to activate.uhc.com and click "Sign In". Enter your registered email and password to access your account dashboard.

UCard Benefits

  • Check OTC and utility benefit balance
  • Use as a digital ID at clinics and hospitals
  • Track health coverage and plan details
  • Find in-network providers and pharmacies

Card Activation Steps

  1. Visit activate.uhc.com
  2. Log in or register
  3. Click "Activate UCard"
  4. Follow on-screen instructions to complete activation
